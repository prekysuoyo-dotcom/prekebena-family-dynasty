Mission Statement

To foster a supportive, loving family where every Prekebena feels valued, empowered, and motivated to contribute to the collective well‑being and sustainable development of the family.

Vision Statement

To be the leading family name in our region, recognised for unity, the love of God, financial resilience, and the active participation in shaping a prosperous future for the Prekebena name.

Core Values

  • Love & Respect – Treat every member with dignity.
  • Integrity – Honest dealings in all financial and relational matters.
  • Collaboration – Work together across ages and branches.
  • Commitment – Fulfil obligations promptly and responsibly.
  • Continuous Improvement – Learn, adapt, and grow as a family.

Motto: “Together we rise, together we thrive.”

Slogan: "United we stand, prosperous we grow."
