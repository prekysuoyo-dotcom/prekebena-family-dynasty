To be the leading family name in our region, recognised for unity, the love of God, financial resilience, and active participation in shaping a prosperous future for the Prekebena name.

Family Structure & Membership

General Assembly: Approve budgets, elect Board, amend bylaws.

Board of Directors: Strategic direction, oversee finances, represent the family.

Executive Committee: Execute Board decisions, day-to-day operations.

Committees: Welfare, Youth, Audit, PRO, etc.

Eligibility: Any Prekebena aged 18+, must sign membership agreement, pay initial registration.

Rights: Vote in General Assembly, access loans, attend meetings/events.

Obligations: Uphold family values, pay contributions, participate in activities, respect hierarchy.

Registration Fee: ₦5,000 (one-time)

Monthly Contribution: ₦2,000

Special Dues: As needed (burial, marriage, community projects)

Savings/Share Purchase: Optional, min ₦5,000 monthly

Penalties: ₦500 surcharge for late payments

AGM: Once a year, last quarter.

Monthly Executive Meeting: First Sunday 3–6pm

Special Meetings: Called by President or 1/3 of members.

Agenda: Opening prayer, roll call, previous minutes, reports, announcements, closing prayer.
